Chocolate Chip Cookie Chocolate Cupcake Strawberry Napoleons

Ingredients

  • 3.5 ounce instant vanilla pudding and pie filling
  • 1 cup cold 2% milk
  • 1 1/2 cups non-dairy whipped topping, thawed
  • 17.25 ounce frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 pint fresh strawberries, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup confectioners' sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Combine pudding mix and cold milk; fold in whipped topping. Refrigerate until ready to use.
  3. Unfold pastry on a cool, lightly floured surface. Cut into 3 strips along fold marks, then cut each strip into four equal pieces. Place 2 inches apart on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown. Remove from baking sheet, and cool.
  5. Split pastries into 2 layers, setting the 8 best looking tops aside. Spread 8 bottom layers with dollops of the pudding mixture. Top each with a layer of strawberries, a small amount of pudding, and another pastry layer.
  6. Spread with remaining pudding mixture and strawberries, and top with remaining pastry layers (the best looking ones). Sprinkle with confectioner's sugar.
